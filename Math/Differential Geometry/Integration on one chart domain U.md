$$
\int_U f:= \int_{y(U)} d^d\beta\quad \omega_{(y)}(y^{-1}(\beta))f_{(y)}(\beta).
$$

We show that this definiton is well defined by considering the two charts $(U,x)$ and $(U,y)$:
$$
\begin{aligned}
\int_U f&:= \int_{y(U)} d^d\beta\quad \omega_{(y)}(y^{-1}(\beta))f_{(y)}(\beta)\\
