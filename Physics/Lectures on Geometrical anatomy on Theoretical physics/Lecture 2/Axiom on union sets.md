### Axiom
Let $x$ be a set. Then there exists a set, $u$, whose elements are precisely the elements of the elements of $x$.

$$
\forall x:\exists u:a\in u\iff a\in b\in x.
$$
We denote set $u$ as 
$$
u = \bigcup x.
$$
#### Examples
Let $x = \{\{a\},\{b\}\}$. Then 
$$
\bigcup x = \{a,b\}.
$$
And $\bigcup x$ is a set.
